This Privacy Policy describes how TheRyanStudio ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the Mediary app ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or account credentials. However, third-party services integrated into the App may collect certain data as described below.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data on our behalf:

3. Files You Import

Any files (PDFs, EPUBs, videos) you import into the App are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or access your files.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time lifetime purchase for premium access. Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data directly, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Any data collected by third-party SDKs is subject to their respective retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
